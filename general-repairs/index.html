Frequently Asked Questions

How often should I service my car?

Most vehicles need a service every 10,000 km or 6 months — whichever comes first. In WA conditions with high heat and dust, more frequent servicing can extend the life of your vehicle significantly.

What is included in a logbook service?

A logbook service follows the manufacturer's schedule — typically covering oil and filter, air filter, spark plugs, brake fluid, coolant, tyre checks and a full vehicle inspection. We stamp your logbook so your warranty stays valid.

Do you service all makes and models?

Yes — Toyota, Mazda, Ford, Hyundai, Nissan, Honda, Mitsubishi, Kia, Subaru, European vehicles, 4WDs and light commercial vehicles. If you drive it, we can service it.

How long does a general service take?

A standard service typically takes 1.5 to 2.5 hours. We will give you an accurate timeframe when you book and keep you updated if anything else needs attention.
